SkinLesionAI is an AI-powered platform for skin lesion classification. Our models are trained on high-quality datasets to accurately identify various types of skin conditions.

Datasets

  • HAM10000: 7 skin lesion classes including Actinic Keratoses, Basal Cell Carcinoma, Melanoma, and more.
  • Massive Clinical Dataset: 10 classes including malignant lesions, benign tumors, vascular tumors, acne, eczema, psoriasis, and contact dermatitis.

üéØ Motivation

Inspired by the idea of exploring how AI can help analyze both normal and abnormal skin lesions, not only cancerous ones. The goal is to show how AI can support researchers, students, and developers in dermatology.

HAM10000 (EfficientNet)and HAM10000 (ConvNeXt): predicts 7 classes ‚Äì Actinic Keratoses (akiec), Basal Cell Carcinoma (bcc), Benign Keratosis-like Lesions (bkl), Dermatofibroma (df), Melanoma (mel), Melanocytic Nevi (nv), and Vascular Lesions (vasc) using a 5-fold ensemble for improved accuracy.

Massive Dataset (ConvNeXt): predicts 10 clinical classes ‚Äì Actinic Keratosis & other malignant lesions, Malignant, Melanoma Skin Cancer, Nevi & Moles, Seborrheic Keratoses & other benign tumors, Vascular Tumors, Warts/Molluscum/Other viral infections, Acne & Rosacea, Eczema, Psoriasis/Lichen Planus/Related Diseases, Poison Ivy/Other Contact Dermatitis.

Grad-CAM visualizations are used to highlight regions influencing predictions, helping to make AI decisions more interpretable.
