SkinLesionAI is an AI-powered platform for skin lesion classification. Our models are trained on high-quality datasets to accurately identify various types of skin conditions.

Datasets

  • HAM10000: 7 skin lesion classes including Actinic Keratoses, Basal Cell Carcinoma, Melanoma, and more.
  • Massive Clinical Dataset: 10 classes including malignant lesions, benign tumors, vascular tumors, acne, eczema, psoriasis, and contact dermatitis.

We thank the contributors of HAM10000 and the Massive Clinical Dataset for providing essential data, and Hugging Face for their tools and infrastructure. Special thanks to mentors and the open-source community for their support.
